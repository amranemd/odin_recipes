lasagnia recipe

Descripstion

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of flat pasta cheets

rich meat , or vegi sauce

Ingredients
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 midium onion
  • 2-3 cloves garlic , minced
  • 800g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tabelspoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oli
step by step to make the best pasta
      PREPARE THE MEAT SAUCE
    1. heat oil
    2. cook the onion and garlic
    3. brown the meat
    4. add tomatoes
    5. simmer
      PREHEAT OVER
    1. preheat your oven to 180c
      ASSEMBLE THE LASAGNA
    1. layer 1 - meat sauce
    2. layer 2 - pasta cheets
    3. layer 3 - cheese
    4. repeat
      BAKE THE LASAGNA
    1. cover
    2. bake
    3. uncover
      REST AND SERVE
    1. let lasagna rest for 10 min before cutting into it
